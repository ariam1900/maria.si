Hi, I am María.

I studied Interactive Design and Audiovisual and Multimedia Production in Bogotá, Colombia.

I’m passionate about creating immersive storytelling experiences. This allows me to connect my two careers, take the viewer to the next level of immersion by changing their role from viewer to user, and convey the message more effectively and powerfully.

My work covers photography, video production, VR / AR / XR, web design, UI / UX, among others. I constantly update my knowledge and have a wide range of interests.

For me it is very important to help others with my work and to support the development of individuals and communities. I like to share my knowledge and generate content that activates reflection in the people who interact with it.

I’m an organized and proactive person who enjoys working in a team and adapts easily. In my free time I like to be active, traveling, listening to music and playing sports.

Feel free to check my resume or contact me for more information.
